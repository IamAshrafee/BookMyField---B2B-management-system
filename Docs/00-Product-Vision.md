# 00 — Product Vision

# BookMyField Product Vision

## 1. Product Name

**BookMyField**

## 2. One-Line Vision

BookMyField is a smart turf booking and management system that helps sports field owners manage bookings, slots, payments, customers, staff, and daily operations from one simple dashboard.

## 3. Product Summary

BookMyField is a web-based management system designed for turf owners, sports field businesses, football ground owners, futsal courts, cricket nets, and similar sports facilities.

In Bangladesh, many turf businesses still manage bookings through phone calls, WhatsApp messages, Facebook Messenger, notebooks, Excel sheets, or staff memory. This often creates confusion, double bookings, payment tracking problems, missed slots, and poor customer experience.

BookMyField aims to solve these problems by creating a centralized system where turf owners and staff can manage real-time bookings, multiple fields, customer information, payments, pricing, reports, and business operations in one place.

The first version of BookMyField will focus on helping turf businesses manage their internal operations smoothly. Later, the product can expand into a customer-facing booking platform or marketplace where players can discover and book nearby turfs online.

## 4. Product Purpose

The main purpose of BookMyField is to make turf business management easier, faster, and more accurate.

Turf owners should not depend only on phone calls, manual notes, or staff memory to manage their business. They need a system that clearly shows which slots are available, which slots are booked, who booked them, how much payment has been received, how much is due, and how the business is performing.

BookMyField will help owners reduce operational mistakes, increase booking efficiency, improve customer experience, and understand their revenue better.

## 5. Problem Statement

Turf businesses in Bangladesh are growing quickly, but many of them still use manual systems to manage daily operations.

A typical turf owner faces problems such as:

* Double booking the same time slot.
* Losing booking information from phone calls or messages.
* Not knowing which customer paid advance and which customer has due payment.
* Staff making mistakes while handling bookings.
* No real-time view of available and booked slots.
* No proper customer database.
* No easy way to track daily, weekly, or monthly revenue.
* No clear report on peak hours, cancelled bookings, or no-show customers.
* Customers needing to call or message again and again just to check availability.
* Owners depending too much on manual communication.

These problems waste time, reduce trust, create customer dissatisfaction, and limit business growth.

## 6. Target Users

BookMyField will mainly serve two types of users.

### 6.1 Primary Users

The primary users are turf owners and sports facility managers who operate one or more sports fields.

They need a reliable system to manage:

* Bookings
* Fields
* Slots
* Payments
* Customers
* Staff
* Reports
* Daily operations

### 6.2 Secondary Users

The secondary users are staff members, receptionists, booking managers, and accountants who help run the turf business.

They need a simple dashboard where they can:

* Add bookings
* Update booking status
* Check available slots
* Collect payments
* View customer details
* Manage cancellations
* Report daily activity to the owner

### 6.3 Future Users

In the future, BookMyField can also serve players and teams who want to book turf slots online.

They may use the system to:

* Search nearby turfs
* Check available time slots
* Book a slot
* Pay advance
* View booking confirmation
* Cancel or reschedule bookings
* Save favorite turfs

However, the MVP will focus mainly on turf owners and staff.

## 7. Target Market

The initial target market is Bangladesh, especially cities and areas where turf football and sports field businesses are popular.

Possible target locations include:

* Dhaka
* Chattogram
* Sylhet
* Cumilla
* Rajshahi
* Khulna
* Barishal
* Cox’s Bazar
* University areas
* Residential areas
* Sports club zones

The product can later expand to other South Asian markets where similar sports facility booking businesses exist.

## 8. Product Goals

The main goals of BookMyField are:

### 8.1 Operational Goal

Help turf owners manage all bookings and field operations from one dashboard.

### 8.2 Business Goal

Help turf businesses increase confirmed bookings, reduce missed opportunities, and improve revenue tracking.

### 8.3 Customer Experience Goal

Make it easier for customers to check availability and book slots without unnecessary calls or confusion.

### 8.4 Management Goal

Give owners clear visibility into daily bookings, payments, revenue, staff activity, and business performance.

### 8.5 Growth Goal

Start as a turf management SaaS product and later expand into a larger sports booking platform.

## 9. Product Objectives

The first version of BookMyField should achieve the following objectives:

1. Allow turf owners to create and manage their business profile.
2. Allow owners to add multiple branches and fields.
3. Allow staff to create, update, cancel, and manage bookings.
4. Prevent double booking for the same field and time slot.
5. Show real-time slot availability.
6. Track customer details and booking history.
7. Track payment status, advance payment, full payment, and due amount.
8. Allow different pricing for different times, days, and fields.
9. Generate basic reports for daily and monthly revenue.
10. Provide a simple public booking page for customers.
11. Keep the system simple enough for non-technical turf owners and staff.

## 10. Product Positioning

BookMyField will be positioned as:

**A complete booking and business management system for turf owners and sports field businesses.**

It is not just a booking calendar. It is an operations system that helps owners manage the full business flow.

BookMyField will focus on:

* Simplicity
* Real-time booking
* Payment tracking
* Staff control
* Business reporting
* Local business needs
* Bangladesh-friendly workflows

## 11. Unique Value Proposition

BookMyField helps turf owners stop managing bookings manually and start running their turf business with a clear, organized, and real-time system.

Instead of using phone calls, WhatsApp messages, notebooks, and Excel sheets separately, owners can manage everything from one dashboard.

The key value is:

**Less confusion, fewer booking mistakes, better payment tracking, and smoother business operation.**

## 12. Core Product Features

The core product will include:

### 12.1 Booking Management

Owners and staff can manage all bookings from a calendar or slot-based view.

They can:

* Add manual bookings
* Accept online booking requests
* Update booking status
* Cancel bookings
* Block unavailable slots
* View upcoming bookings
* View past bookings

### 12.2 Real-Time Slot Availability

The system will show available, booked, pending, cancelled, and blocked slots in real time.

This will help prevent double booking and make slot management easier.

### 12.3 Multiple Field Management

A turf business may have one field or multiple fields. BookMyField will support multiple fields under one business or branch.

Example:

* Field 1 — Football 5v5
* Field 2 — Football 7v7
* Field 3 — Cricket Net
* Field 4 — Indoor Futsal

### 12.4 Customer Management

The system will store customer information such as:

* Name
* Phone number
* Team name
* Booking history
* Payment history
* No-show history
* Notes

This will help owners identify regular customers and manage repeat bookings.

### 12.5 Payment Tracking

The system will track booking payment status.

Payment statuses may include:

* Unpaid
* Advance paid
* Fully paid
* Partially paid
* Refunded
* Due
* Cancelled

### 12.6 Pricing Management

Owners can set different prices based on:

* Field
* Sport type
* Day
* Time
* Peak hour
* Off-peak hour
* Weekend
* Special event
* Custom booking

### 12.7 Staff Management

Owners can invite staff members and assign roles.

Possible roles:

* Owner
* Manager
* Staff
* Accountant

Each role will have different permissions.

### 12.8 Reports and Dashboard

The dashboard will show important business information such as:

* Today’s bookings
* Today’s revenue
* Pending payments
* Due amount
* Available slots
* Cancelled bookings
* Monthly revenue
* Top booked fields
* Peak booking hours

### 12.9 Public Booking Page

Each turf business can have a public booking page where customers can view available slots and request bookings.

Example:

`BookMyField.com/green-arena`

Customers can:

* Select date
* Select field
* Select time slot
* Enter contact information
* Submit booking request
* Receive confirmation

## 13. MVP Vision

The MVP version of BookMyField should not try to become a full marketplace immediately.

The first goal is to build a strong internal management system for turf owners.

The MVP should focus on:

* Owner login
* Business setup
* Branch and field setup
* Slot calendar
* Manual booking
* Booking conflict prevention
* Customer database
* Payment status tracking
* Basic dashboard
* Public booking page
* Basic reports

The MVP should be simple, useful, and practical for real turf owners.

## 16. Product Principles

BookMyField should be built with the following principles:

### 16.1 Simplicity First

The system must be easy for non-technical owners and staff to use.

### 16.2 Real Business Workflow

The product should match how turf businesses actually work in Bangladesh.

### 16.3 Mobile-Friendly Design

Many owners and staff will use the system from mobile devices, so the interface must work well on mobile.

### 16.4 Fast Booking Experience

Adding or checking a booking should be quick and simple.

### 16.5 No Double Booking

The system must strongly prevent booking conflicts.

### 16.6 Clear Payment Tracking

Owners should always know who paid, who has due payment, and how much money was collected.

### 16.7 Scalable Structure

The system should be designed in a way that can later support multiple branches, multiple sports, online payments, and marketplace features.
